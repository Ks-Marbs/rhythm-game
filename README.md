## FAQ:
### Why not WASD/Arrows?
FVNJ or DFJK are the default hand positions for rhythm games, where your hands are at rest, notice that both F and J usually have markings
### Why can’t we change keys?
Working on it, but that would involve… checks notes 250 new sprites
### Why is it square?

### Why are the colors dull?
As a rhythm game player, I know most games use very saturated colors, but that does harm the eyes after awhile, especially since in hard sections some people don’t even dare blinking
### Why no penalty?
The game is supposed to be easier to understand, in fact, its rare for rhythm games to punish wrong keys, plus it would take an unnecessary amount of time to program that
### Can I edit this?
That's the whole point! Feel free to make this better or switch it to a full blown game, I'd be happy to recieve credit, and please do not copy the charts, but except that, it's fully editable
### Why is the code a bit confusing?
This was not the plan, and I am working on better explanations that also do not bloat the files, but a simple answer is that EVERY single scene is connected, so you may need to figure out what is connected to what, for example, the menu scene spawns leveltaps which those set the level before going to lvl scene, which then spawns fnotes and helds 

### For me, I think making a code that's easy to follow is very hard, I did my best but considering many files talk with each other, that makes it harder to follow
